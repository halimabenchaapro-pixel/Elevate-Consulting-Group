Our Story

Built by Business People, for Business People

Elevate Consulting Group was founded in 2009 by a group of former operators who were tired of receiving consulting advice from people who had never actually run a business. We built Elevate to be different.

Today, we're a team of 40+ consultants, analysts, and specialists who have collectively founded, scaled, and exited companies across technology, finance, retail, and professional services.

We don't write 200-page slide decks. We sit beside your team, identify what's blocking growth, and build the systems and strategies to move you forward — fast.
